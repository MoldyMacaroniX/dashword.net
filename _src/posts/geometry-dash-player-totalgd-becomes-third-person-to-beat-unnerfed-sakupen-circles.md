Geometry Dash player Totalgd has become the third ever person to complete unnerfed Sakupen Circles.

This comes only [four days after](https://youtu.be/s0hCB_KtJ5k?si=rAE30HjfcX3kFzhN) his 83% run that took her more than 630,000 attempts. But now, after an additional 20,000 attempts, Totalgd has [finally completed the level](https://x.com/DashwordGD/status/2043332120928559192).

[Sakupen Circles](/posts/sakupen-circles-top-1/) is already on the [Demon List](/categories/demonlist/), but that is the nerfed version. The version Totalgd completed is the [unnerfed version](https://youtu.be/TY3DB4M8tgA).

This is incredible, as the whole reason the level was nerfed in the first place was because it was believed to be impossible. But now, a third player has beaten it, proving the Geometry Dash skill ceiling has still not been reached.

Because this level is not rated in-game, it will not appear on the [Pointercrate Demon List](/posts/geometry-dash-demon-list-where-to-find-the-hardest-demons/), meaning we are unsure where it would place relative to other top demons. But it without a doubt would place high.
