Geometry Dash player totalgd has become the third ever person to complete unnerfed Sakupen Circles.

This comes only [four days after](https://youtu.be/s0hCB_KtJ5k?si=rAE30HjfcX3kFzhN) her 83% run that took more than 630,000 attempts. But now, after an additional 20,000 attempts, **totalgd** has [finally completed the level](https://x.com/DashwordGD/status/2043332120928559192).

[Sakupen Circles](/posts/sakupen-circles-top-1/) is currently #30 on the [Demon List](/categories/demonlist/), but that is the nerfed version. The version totalgd completed is the [unnerfed version](https://youtu.be/TY3DB4M8tgA), and because the level is not rated in-game, it can not be added to [Pointercrate](/posts/geometry-dash-demon-list-where-to-find-the-hardest-demons/). However, it is believed to be around Top 10 difficulty, as it sits at #3 on the [Unrated Demons List](https://udl.pages.dev/#/).

This is incredible, as the whole reason the level was nerfed in the first place was because it was believed to be impossible. But now, three people have proven that to be wrong, showing that the Geometry Dash skill ceiling is still far from being reached.
