Viprin, a Geometry Dash moderator and community manager, has announced his departure from Geometry Dash after 12 years. He states he wants to step down to pursue "other opportunities."

He says he will be be resigning from his moderator position in Geometry Dash, shut down his Discord server, stop uploading to his YouTube channel, and disable comments on his YouTube videos. He also says he will leave private community spaces he is a part of.

This comes as a shock and with no prior warning. As such, it is unclear why Viprin is choosing to step down now. Because he gave very little reason, many suspect this is a result of the controversies he has been involved in over the years. But Viprin also stated that he was "not motivated to create" anymore.

# Viprin's Legacy

[Viprin said that his achievements](https://www.youtube.com/post/UgkxSzDOuUJomwRkS766bR9a3tXHNQ-HMJf3) "include 8 GD Awards, 9 Viprin Creator Contests, 9 Gauntlet Contests, 2 Icon Contests, 26 Discord Server Contests, 5 Mini Creator Contests, the Sparky bot, the GD 10-Year Anniversary (GD10) video, and more recently the 2.2 Sneak Peek, the NCS collaboration and other Artist Reveals, as well as setting up Event Level songs and creators (with many still in progress) and picking Gauntlet Levels." Viprin was also the [top creator on the in-game creators leaderboard for years](/posts/top-1-geometry-dash-creator-viprin-has-been-dethroned/).

But Viprin is also a controversial figure. While responsible for hosting many Geometry Dash events and creator contests, he has been criticized for [inappropriate conduct](/posts/geometry-dash-elder-moderator-viprin-exposed-for-a-lot-of-things-actually/). Many also dislike the direction he took Geometry Dash in.

People [praise him for being responsible for taking Geometry Dash in the direction it is headed now](https://x.com/dreameatergd/status/2041541852180951254). He is believed to be largely responsible for making the moderator system what it is now and for creating many events that are now officially endorsed by RobTop.

However, there are those who dislike the bureaucratic direction Viprin took Geometry Dash in. He is criticized for making all these decisions himself and for [creating private, groupthink environments like Mod Chat](/posts/geometry-dash-hack-exposes-controversial-conduct-within-mod-team/) for people to confirm his decisions. Danzmen, for one, criticized the moderator team for consisting of "toxicity, power trips, deep-seated insecurity."

**But regardless of whether or not you love or hate Viprin, to say he is either all perfect or all evil is misleading.** The situation is nuance. He created many beloved community events and no doubt helped the game a lot. But he also made many mistakes.

As [this Twitter/X user](https://x.com/ItsAndreXtar/status/2041538794483044380) points out:

> While Viprin isn't a saint as such (you know what I'm talking about), it doesn't take away from the fact that he helped keep the game and the community going for years without the 2.2 update, the question is... what will GD and its community become without Viprin...?

# Going Forward

Viprin said that despite the fact that he wanted to finish his megacollab "Astra" to be one final send-off, he said he doesn't want to force it, as he doesn't have the motivation to create anymore. Instead, it will be finished and uploaded by Floppy.

As for community events like the Geometry Dash Awards and creator contests, it is unclear what future form they will take without Viprin. It is possible other community members will host these events and they will continue as usual. The current [Duality creator contest](https://youtu.be/nDBhWpen-eU?si=qf0vsryTeDVws5H_) is also still ongoing even without Viprin.

---

Regardless of your opinion on Viprin, his impact on this game is undeniable. What form Geometry Dash will take going forward is uncertain. It is possible other moderators like Pauze or AutoNick will take charge, but there is no official word yet.
