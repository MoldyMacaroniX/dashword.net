Top player and streamer Doggie has died at 97% on the upcoming [top 1 hardest level in Geometry Dash](/posts/new-top-1-geometry-dash-demon-tidal-wave-verified-by-zoink-in-50000-attempts/) "Grief" after over 400,000 attempts and 360+ streams. This comes after an 84% fail back in May.

This very well could have been the run Doggie beat it. This could have been a new top 1 verification with a fluke from 84%, but it wasn't meant to be.

Grief is an upcoming top 1 hardest demon in Geometry Dash made by Icedcave and a [talented group of creators](https://youtu.be/bQXnREyaO34). This is a remake of the 2015 version of [Slaughterhouse](/posts/breaking-acheron-takes-1-spot-on-geometry-dash-demonlist/), a former top 1 demon in Geometry Dash that was also verified by Doggie after [SpaceUK was exposed for hacking his completion](/posts/top-1-geometry-dash-player-spaceuk-exposed-for-hacking-achievements/).

Doggie went live on his account to verify Grief for the first time in May 2024. After over two years, Doggie is closer than ever to finally beating the level. Once he verifies Grief, it is expected to place at the top 1 spot on the [Geometry Dash Demon List](/categories/demonlist/).

Hopefully Doggie will complete this level soon and a new era of Geometry Dash difficulty will begin!
