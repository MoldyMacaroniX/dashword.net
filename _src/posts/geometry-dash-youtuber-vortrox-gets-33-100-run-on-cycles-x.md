Earlier today, famous Geometry Dash YouTuber and Streamer Vortrox achieved a 33-100% run on Cycles X, his [upcoming extreme demon](/categories/demonlist/) remake of Cycles.

This comes after two months of continuously grinding the level live on stream. [Vortrox also noted](https://x.com/vortroxx/status/2043348284480057773) this run came after "10 streams of no progress."

This means Vortrox has completed the latter two thirds of the level in a single attempt. Now, all he needs to do is complete this run again, but from the start of the level. This is easier said than done but this run shows Vortrox is capable.

With Vortrox being so close to verifying the level now, he will likely verify the level soon and release the level for others to play.
